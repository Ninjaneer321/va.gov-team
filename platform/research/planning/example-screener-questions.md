# Example Screener Questions

### General Experience Screening

**`1.` Have you visited a VA urgent care facility in the last 6 months?**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes

**`2.` Do you currently have an active disability claim with the VA?**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes

**`3.` In the last 12 months, have you used VA.gov to manage your health care? This might include scheduling appointments, requesting prescription refills, or sending secure messages to your care team.**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes

---

### Assistive Technology Screening

**`4.` Do you regularly use any assistive technology to access websites or mobile apps? This includes screen readers (like JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, or TalkBack), screen magnification, voice control, or switch devices.**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes, I regularly use [specific technology]
- **Follow-up if YES:** Please specify which assistive technology you use and approximately how long you've been using it.

**`5.` Which screen reader do you primarily use to navigate websites?**
- **Qualifying response:** 
  - JAWS
  - NVDA  
  - VoiceOver (Mac/iOS)
  - TalkBack (Android)
  - Other: [specify]

**`6.` How would you rate your experience level with [screen reader name from Q5]?**
- **Qualifying response:** 
  - Beginner (less than 6 months)
  - Intermediate (6 months to 2 years)  
  - Advanced (more than 2 years)
- **Preferred:** Intermediate or Advanced

**`7.` Do you use screen magnification or zoom features to view content on your computer or mobile device?**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes, I use magnification at [X%] or higher

**`8.` What device do you typically use with your assistive technology?**
- **Qualifying response:** 
  - Windows computer
  - Mac computer
  - iPhone
  - Android phone
  - iPad
  - Other: [specify]

**`9.` Are you willing to share your screen and allow researchers to observe you using your assistive technology during a remote session?**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes

---

#### Cognitive and Other Disability Screening

**`10.` Do you have a cognitive disability or consideration? This includes conditions like traumatic brain injury (TBI), PTSD, memory loss, or attention difficulties.**
- **Qualifying response:** Yes
- **Note:** Follow [research with cognitive disabilities best practices](https://depo-platform-documentation.scrollhelp.site/research-design/research-with-assistive-technology-users)
