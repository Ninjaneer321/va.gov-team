## Task-based feedback

**Total responses:**
- Key themes
  - Forms and submission issues (31)
  - Technical and sign-in issues with the website (2)
  - Can't complete task (not form related) (5)

### Forms and submission issues
This is the most prevalent category. The main issue is not knowing the address to send a form to or not being able to access a specifc form (1095-B) 
Total responses: 31
- Common issues:
  - Forms not having a clear submission address
  - Difficulty in downloading or filling out forms
  - Problems with specific forms not working (1095-B)

### Can't complete task (not form related)
Total responses: 5
- Issues:
  - Reorder CPAP equipment
  - Find a fax number
  - Set up an appointment with a VAMC
    
### Technical and sign-in issues with the website
Total responses: 2
- Issues:
  - Problem with sign-in
