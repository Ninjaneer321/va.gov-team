# Results Page 2.0: UAT Plan

## Background

Results Page 2.0 usability testing revealed usability issues and areas for improvement, which we have implemented into the product in staging.

- **Product location:** Discover Your Benefits (staging)
- **User familiarity:** Iteration - Improvements to Discover Your Benefits
- **Product brief:** https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/tree/master/products/identity-personalization/my-va/2022-audit

## Research design

- **Methodology:** User Acceptance Testing (UAT) with usability validation
- **Format:** Internal usability testing
- **Location:** Remote
- **Moderated:** Yes

## Research goals

1. Confirm that all functionality works as expected with updates (except for the known workaround for filter collapse on mobile in Safari).
2. Verify that all sorting and filtering work as expected, with special attention to mobile.
3. Confirm that redesigned content works as expected with no usability issues.

## Research questions

- Does the functionality work as expected?
- Are there significant unknown usability issues?

## Hypotheses

- Veterans will not encounter any unknown bugs on Discover Your Benefits.
- Veterans will be able to easily navigate sort, filter, and benefit content features on the Results page.

## Expected outcomes

Based on the outcome of this research study, we will either:

- release the updates to production, and/or
- update our design to correct any significant usability hurdles people encounter.

- **Approach:** Internal recruitment
- **Recruiting partner:** Perigean (if needed)

- **Session length:** 45 minutes
