# Results Page 2.0: UAT Plan

## Background

The Results Page 2.0 usability study revealed usability issues and areas for improving the Discover Your Benefits tool, which we have implemented into the product in staging. All implemented changes will be testing during UAT sessions to ensure usabilty.
- **Product location:** [Discover Your Benefits in staging](https://staging.va.gov/discover-your-benefits/introduction)
- **User familiarity:** Iteration - Improvements to Discover Your Benefits
- **Product brief:** [Product brief](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/tree/master/products/vet-transition-support/results-page-2.0)
- **Conversation guide:** [Conversation guide](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/blob/master/products/vet-transition-support/results-page-2.0/research/UAT/results-page-2.0-UAT-conversation-guide.md)

## Research design

- **Methodology:** User Acceptance Testing (UAT) with usability validation
- **Format:** Internal usability testing
- **Location:** Remote
- **Moderated:** Yes

## Research goals

1. Confirm that all product functionality works as expected (except for known issue caused by the bug workaround for filter area collapse on mobile).
2. Verify that all sorting and filtering work as expected, with special attention to mobile.
3. Confirm that redesigned questionnaire and benefit content performs as expected.

## Research questions

- Does all product functionality work as expected on both desktop and mobile?
- Are there significant unknown usability issues?
- Are all UI and content updates functioning as intended?

## Hypotheses

- Veterans will not encounter any unknown usability issues or bugs on Discover Your Benefits.
- Veterans will be able to easily navigate sort, filter, and benefit content features on the Results page.
- Updated content performs well.

## Expected outcomes

Based on the outcome of this research study, we will either:

- release the updates to production, and/or
- update our design to correct any significant usability hurdles people encounter.

## Recruitment & participants

- **Approach:** Internal recruitment
- **Recruiting partner:** None

## Timeline & sessions
