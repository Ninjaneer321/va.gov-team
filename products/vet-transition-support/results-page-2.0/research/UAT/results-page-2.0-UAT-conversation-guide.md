# Conversation Guide for UAT  

This study evaluates Results Page 2.0 for usability. These participants were recruited internally instead of our standard recruitment through Perigean. Because these participants are technically savvy and understand the testing process, we have shortened the introductory and technical setup sections to focus directly on the core tasks.

## Moderator resources

- Product in staging: Product link
- Notetaking spreadsheet: Notes doc

## Intro

Thanks for joining today. I'm Anna, and I've got [NAME] who will be taking notes.

A few logistics:
- Discover Your Benefits is designed for Transitioning Service Members and recently separated Veterans to discover and explore benefits in a simple, and centralized way. 
- You do not need to sign in.
- I'm going to send you a link to the product in staging in a minute.

### Scenario 1 (RSV)
- For this UAT session, I would like you to role play as a Veteran in your first year post-separation from military service. You have gone through the process of applying for Disability benefits. You have an honorable discharge. All other decisions using the product are your own. 

### Scenario 2 (TSM)
- For this UAT session, I would like you to role play as a Transitioning Service Member in your last year of service. You are completely new to VA.gov. You anticipate receiving an honorable discharge. All other decisions using the product are your own.
