* Thank you so much for joining today. My name is Risha, and \[NAME\] here will be taking notes.  
* Today, we‚Äôll be discussing how people think about and manage payments. I‚Äôll ask you some questions about your experiences making payments with the VA and other institutions. # **Payment behaviors (25 minutes)**

* Switching gears to talk about your experience with the VA. I‚Äôd like to ask you some questions about how you make payments. I want to make sure that you‚Äôre only sharing what you‚Äôre willing to share.  
* Tell me about the last time you paid a bill to the VA.  
  * How did you do it?  
  * What was easy? What was frustrating?  
* What would have made that experience easier?  
* Think about a recent payment you made that felt easier?   
  * Tell me about that experience. What made it easier than the VA?  
* Have you ever been on a payment plan with the VA?  
  * Tell me about that experience. How did you set it up?  
  * How did you decide on the amount and timeline?  
  * What was easy? What was difficult?  
  * Would you do anything differently next time?  
* How did that compare to a payment plan outside the VA, if you‚Äôve had one?

\[Moderator‚Äôs note: ask if participant says they haven‚Äôt been on a VA payment plan\]

* Have you ever been on a payment plan outside of the VA?  
  * Tell me about that experience. How did you set it up?  
  * How did you decide on the amount and timeline?  
  * What was easy? What was difficult?  
  * Would you do anything differently next time?  
* Have you ever considered setting up automatic payments for any of your debts?  
  * \[IF YES\] What made you decide to set that up? How did you go about setting that up?  
  * \[IF NO\] What made you decide against it?  
* Let me ask you a bit about how you manage your finances in general. How do you track and manage your finances?  
  * What information matters most for you when you‚Äôre paying bills?  
  * Do you use any tools to track and manage your finances?  
  * How do you make sure you have enough money in your account when payments are due?  
  * What makes you feel secure when making a payment?

* Now we‚Äôll switch gears and I‚Äôll ask for your feedback on a part of the website we‚Äôre working on.
