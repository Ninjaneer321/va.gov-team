## Positive Feedback (350 ratings, 76%)

Veterans frequently describe the app as "easy to use," "helpful," and "well organized," with particular praise for navigation, prescription access, and overall reliability.

**Representative responses:**

- "The app is wonderful. Easy to use and easy to navigate."
- "Whoever developed and maintains this app deserves to be paid more."

---

## Negative Feedback (74 ratings, 16%)

Negative feedback centers on difficulties accessing certain features — especially claims letters, payment history, appointments, and login issues. Some users experienced errors or could not find expected information.

**Representative responses:**

- "I can't find my Claim decision letter."
- "Payment by years not working… I can only read 2023."

---

## Neutral Feedback (36 ratings, 8%)

Neutral feedback generally reflects feature requests or mild usability issues — for example, users wanting more options or functionality similar to the website.

**Representative responses:**

- "I wish the app had more options similar to the website."
- "If there's an easier way to… remove prescriptions from the refill list, that would help."

---

## Common Themes

### 1. Login Difficulties (~20 responses)

Some Veterans reported issues with biometric login or being unexpectedly locked out.

**Representative responses:**

- "Always login issues when I have biometrics enabled."
- "It is constantly locking you out and does not recognize credentials."

**Recommendation:** Investigate biometric login interactions and credential timeouts; consider improving error messaging and session handling.

### 2. Prescription Refill Clarity (~15 responses)

Users struggled to determine whether prescriptions were in process, already shipped, or needed action.

**Representative responses:**

- "I am unable to determine if there is a refill on the way…"
- "Keep it simple… remove it from the refill list."

**Recommendation:** Enhance refill status visibility and reduce ambiguity by differentiating between "shipped," "pending," and "available for refill."

### 3. Payment History / Letters Unavailable or Unclear (~10 responses)

Veterans could not locate key documents or payment information for certain years.

**Representative responses:**

- "Can only read 2023… can't get 2024 or 2025."
- "I can't find my Claim decision letter."

**Recommendation:** Audit data loading for historical documents and add clearer pathways or in-app guidance for locating letters/payments.

---

## User Pain Points

### I. Login Failures and Biometric Issues (20+ reviews)

Veterans repeatedly mentioned login lockouts or biometric login malfunctions.

**Recommendation:** Improve biometric integration, reduce forced logouts, and update prompts to ensure clarity about login errors.

### II. Prescription Status Confusion (15+ reviews)

Users cannot easily understand refill states or shipment status.

**Recommendation:** Refine UI labels and introduce clearer timelines or notifications.

### III. Missing or Inaccessible Claims/Payment Documents (10+ reviews)

Some Veterans expected documents (letters, payment history) that were not visible or appeared outdated.

**Recommendation:** Review backend sync processes and highlight alternative access points if certain data is temporarily unavailable.
