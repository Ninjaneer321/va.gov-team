## Product Overview

Veterans, service members, and surviving spouses who are eligible for a
VA-backed home loan or Native American Direct Loan (NADL) may already
have a VA-issued Certificate of Eligibility (COE) on file.

A COE confirms to lenders that the individual meets the service-based
requirements for a VA home loan, based on their service history.

Users can view or access their existing COE in the **VA: Health and
Benefits App**.

For Veterans who do not yet have a COE, the application process is
available online through VA.gov.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## User Access

### Who can access this feature?

Active service members and Veterans who have logged in with **Level of
Assurance 3 (LOA3)** --- the highest level of identity verification on
VA.gov --- can access the COE in the VAHB App under **VA Letters and
Documents**.

### How do users log in?

Veterans can log in to the mobile app using:

-   Login.gov\
-   ID.me

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Navigation

Veterans can access their COE letter by:

1.  Selecting **Benefits** from the bottom navigation\
2.  Selecting **VA Letters and Documents**\
3.  Choosing **Review letters**\
4.  Selecting **Certificate of Eligibility -- home loan letter**

The letter will display a **"Now available"** label in the list until
the first time the Veteran selects and views it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Functionality

Veterans will only see the **Certificate of Eligibility** letter in **VA
Letters and Documents** if they are eligible for a COE.

The app: - Does **not** display COE application status\
- Only shows the letter if the user is listed as **eligible** on the
backend

There are two scenarios in which the letter appears:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 1. Eligible + No Application

The user receives an **automatic COE**.

If the user is eligible and VA Loan Guaranty (LGY) has all required
information, an automatic COE is generated.

A banner appears at the top of the screen explaining: - An automatic COE
has been generated\
- No additional information is required

Veterans can select **Review Letter** to download and view their COE.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 2. Eligible + Has Application

The user already has an active COE that they applied for.

The Veteran can select **Review Letter** to download and view their COE
letter.
