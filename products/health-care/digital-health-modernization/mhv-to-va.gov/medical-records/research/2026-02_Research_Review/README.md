# Medical Records Findability Research
**Research Synthesis & Use Case Analysis | February 2026**

---

## What's In This Repository

This folder contains research synthesis and use case analysis for improving Medical Records findability on VA.gov. The work analyzes 16 research sources (2022-2025) to identify pain points, validate insights, and prioritize design opportunities.

**Status:** ✅ Synthesis complete → 🔄 Moving to concept exploration
