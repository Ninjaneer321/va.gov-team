## Top User Pain Points

| Issue | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Imaging buried inside Labs & Tests | Users repeatedly say imaging reports appear in the Labs list, forcing extra scrolling and misdirected searches (e.g., "Where are images from X-rays/Echo?") |
| Missing or incomplete records | Veterans can't find Community Care artifacts, radiology images, EMG/EKG traces, surgical/OR notes, C&P exam documents/DBQs, or see vaccines that "aren't visible." |
| No search/filter and unclear grouping in Medical Records | People report "no search," hidden content, and more paging/clicks to reach basics; labels (e.g., Notes vs. Visit Summaries) feel inconsistent. |
| Download/print workflows are confusing or fail | Users can't locate the download button, don't know where files saved, or encounter timeouts/partial downloads (e.g., "12 hours to download CT, stuck at 38%"). Printing by date range is also harder. |
| Labs: delayed posting, missing reference ranges, no trend view | Complaints include labs not appearing within expected windows (24-72 hours), lack of ranges/comments, and a desire for trend graphs. |
| Clinician notes / visit summaries hard to find or filter by time | Veterans struggle to locate and print notes for a specific period; some ask where a doctor's video visit notes were saved. |
| Vitals and Vaccinations visibility gaps | Vitals trending and Vaccine Records often Missing or Buried |

*Source dataset: Q3, Q4 Medallia Medical Records Surveys*
