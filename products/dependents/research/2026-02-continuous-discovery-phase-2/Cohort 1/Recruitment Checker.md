# Continuous Discovery- Keeping Disabled and Student Dependents Recruitment Checker

For this round our primary criteria were: 
 - At least 7 Veterans who have a disabled dependent on their disability compensation benefit
 - At least 7 Veterans who currently have a student dependent on their disability compensation benefit, have had a student dependent on their disability compensation benefit in the past 2 years, or have a dependent on their disability compensation benefit who will turn 18 in the next 2 years

Our secondary criteria were:
 - At least 7 Veterans who are age 55 or older
 - At least 7 Veterans with a cognitive disability

We met those criteria for completed sessions with Veterans who have/had/will have a student dependent on their disability compensation benefit and Veterans with a cognitive disability.

We were 3 Veterans short for the criteria of at least 7 Veterans who have a disabled dependent on their disability compensation benefit and at least 7 Veterans who age 55 or older.

We also met the recruitement goals for a representative number of Veterans who live in rural areas; women Veterans; Black or African American Veterans; Hispanic, Latino, or Spanish origin Veterans; and Biracial Veterans. 

We did not meet the recruitement goals for a representative number of Veterans who do not have a degree and who are expats. 

We did not track the characteristics of Veterans who are mobile users, Veterans with an other than honorable discharge, Veterans of immigrant origin, and LGBTQ+ Veterans.

We did not recruit any Assistive Technology users.
