# Portal Activity Log

## Overview

VA Notify is building an Activity Log in Portal, which will display the user, date, and what action occurred. This provides Business Contacts and Platform Admins with a detailed, auditable history of changes made within their Service(s).

## Problem Statement

Business Contacts need greater detail on changes occurring within their Service(s) so that they have greater autonomy to self-manage and monitor activity. As more users gain access to Portal, it is important to track what is happening in the system so there is a historical reference on what happened and when.

## Why This Is Important

- As more users are onboarded to Portal, accountability and transparency become critical for safe self-service.
- An activity log supports ATO (Authority to Operate) requirements by providing an auditable record of system changes.
- Improves troubleshooting by surfacing when a template was last touched and by whom.
- Streamlines user management and accountability.

## Solution

Build an Activity Log feature in Portal that records and displays:
- **Who** performed an action (user)
- **When** the action occurred (date/timestamp)
- **What** action was taken (action type and target)

## Desired Outcomes

- Business Contacts can self-manage their Service(s) with greater confidence and autonomy.
- VA Notify team reduces manual support burden related to change investigations.
- ATO compliance is supported through transparent, auditable system activity records.
