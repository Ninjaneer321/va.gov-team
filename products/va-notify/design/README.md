# VA Notify Design

This folder contains design artifacts, guidelines, and templates for VA Notify notifications.

## Notification Channel Guide

The [notification-guide.md](notification-guide.md) helps teams determine whether a notification is appropriate, which channel to use, and what content to include. It covers:
- Suggested user research questions for validating notification need
- Decision framework for choosing between email, SMS, and other channels
- Key notification types with channel recommendations

## Email Frame Template

The [email-frame-template.png](email-frame-template.png) provides a reference layout for VA Notify email notifications, showing the standard VA branding and structural elements.

## Design Principles

VA Notify notifications should:
- Be actionable and timely—deliver the right information at the right moment
- Be channel-appropriate—email for longer or reference-worthy content, SMS for brief and time-sensitive alerts
- Respect Veteran preferences—honor opt-in/opt-out settings managed via VA Profile
- Use plain language consistent with VA.gov content standards
- Include only necessary personalization; avoid PHI/PII in notifications unless the Veteran has explicitly granted permission
